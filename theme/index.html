Benefits of a Balanced HRV

Why is HRV balancing important?

HRV balancing ensures an equal balance between air intake and exhaust, crucial for proper ventilation and system efficiency. It helps prevent pressure imbalances in a home, which can lead to issues like drafts, energy waste, and the introduction of outside contaminants.

Pressure Balance

Balanced intake and exhaust air prevent positive or negative pressure in the home, which can cause air to leakage.

Energy Efficiency

A balanced HRV ensures the system is working optimally, preventing energy waste from excessive intake or exhaust.

Indoor Air Quality

Balanced airflow helps prevent the intrusion of outside pollutants and radon gas, which can negatively impact indoor air quality.

Defrosting

A balanced HRV can properly handle the defrost cycle, which is critical for performance, especially in cold climates.
